Do I need a smartphone?+
Yes — TradesPay AI works on any smartphone browser. No app store download needed. Works on both Android and iPhone.
How does voice invoicing work?+
Tap the mic and speak naturally — "Charge Mike ₦150,000 for HVAC installation, 6 hours labor." Our Qwen AI builds the complete invoice in seconds.
What payment methods can clients use?+
Visa, Mastercard, Google Pay, Apple Pay, bank transfer, USSD, mobile money, and crypto (USDT, BTC, ETH). Powered by Flutterwave.
What is the Lifetime Deal?+
$299 one-time payment — all Premium features forever. No monthly fees ever. Only 20 spots. Once gone, pricing moves to $29-$79/month.

When an invoice goes overdue, AI sends professional reminders at day 7, 14, and 30 — escalating tone automatically. 94% of clients pay after the AI chaser.

Yes. All data is encrypted with SSL/TLS. Your invoices are protected with row-level security — only you can access your data. We never sell your data.

Yes — cancel anytime, no penalties. Lifetime Deal cannot be cancelled because it's a one-time purchase — it's yours forever.
