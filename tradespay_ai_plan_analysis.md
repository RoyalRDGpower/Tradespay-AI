# TradesPay AI: Design Analysis & Implementation Plan

## 1. Understanding the Core Product
**TradesPay AI** is a high-performance sales and invoicing assistant designed for contractors and tradespeople. The goal is to move from "Job Done" to "Getting Paid" with zero friction using AI.

### Key Value Propositions:
*   **Voice-to-Invoice:** Convert natural speech into formatted invoices in seconds.
*   **Photo-to-Invoice:** Use Vision AI to analyze job sites or physical notes and auto-fill line items.
*   **Sales Auto-Pilot:** A critical feature that integrates with Instagram and WhatsApp to automatically respond to customer DMs, answer common questions, and close sales even when the owner is busy on a job.
*   **AI Payment Chaser:** Automatically handles the awkward task of reminding clients to pay.

## 3. Proposed Screen List (5 Screens)

To cover the full scope of your request, I suggest we build the following screens:

1.  **Dashboard (The Command Center):** A high-impact view of revenue, pending payments, and quick-action buttons for Voice/Photo invoicing.
2.  **Auto-Pilot Inbox (The Sales Hub):** A unified inbox for Instagram and WhatsApp DMs where the user can see the AI interacting with customers in real-time.
3.  **Auto-Pilot Configuration:** A dedicated settings page to "train" the AI on the business's services, pricing, and "voice" for automated replies.
4.  **Invoice Generation Flow:** A sleek, step-by-step UI for the Voice and Photo AI processes, showing the "AI Confidence" and allowing quick edits.
5.  **Analytics & Payments:** A professional view showing cash flow trends and a list of all invoices categorized by status (Paid, Overdue, etc.).

---

## 4. Why 5 Screens?
This number allows us to separate the **operational** side (invoicing) from the **growth/sales** side (Auto-Pilot), giving each feature the space it needs to be effective on a mobile screen while keeping the navigation simple for a busy contractor.
